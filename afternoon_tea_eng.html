Afternoon Tea at The Deer & Daisy

In the summer of 2022 we open our tea rooms for our traditional and generous Afternoon Tea.

Dainty, thin sandwiches with delicious fillings. Followed by warm scones with jam and clotted cream and, last but not least, a delightful little cake with a seasonal touch.

Served with lots of tea brewed to perfection and poared from proper tea pots into fine china so think you can almost see straight through it.
