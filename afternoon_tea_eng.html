Afternoon Tea at The Deer & Daisy

On 2nd July 2022 we're opening our "pop-up" Tea Rooms for a traditional and generous Afternoon Tea.

Dainty, thin sandwiches with delicious fillings. Followed by freshly baked scones with jam and clotted cream and, last but not least, a piece of refreshing cake.

Served with lots of tea brewed to perfection and poared from proper tea pots into fine cups. We also serve coffee if that is your preference.

The menu contains gluten and lactose but you may request a vegan alternative on your booking form
