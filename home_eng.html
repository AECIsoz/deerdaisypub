Your Traditional English Pub in Österlen

If this was England ...

... there would be a small, rural, charming, welcoming pub right HERE! The landscape and climate in Österlens makes you think you're in England but there is something missing. We're fixing that. We're creating a traditional English Country Inn in the middle of Kivik.

In the meantime we serve a traditional Afternoon Tea most weekends and a full English Breakfast if you fancy a nice start to your Saturday or Sunday. Or you can rent our holiday cottage and spend a lovely time in beautiful Kivik.
