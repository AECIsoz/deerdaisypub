Afternoon Tea

at The Deer & Daisy

From time to time we open our pop-up tea rooms for a traditional English Afternoon Tea.

Dainty, thin sandwiches with delicious classic fillings. Followed by freshly baked scones with jam and home made clotted cream, just like you want it. Last, but not least, a cake that takes our fancy and will put a smile on your face.

Served with lots of tea brewed to perfection and poared from proper tea pots into fine cups. We also serve coffee if that is your preference.

The menu contains gluten and lactose and you may request a vegetarian alternative on your booking form.
