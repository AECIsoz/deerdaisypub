Christmas Afternoon Tea at The Deer & Daisy

Sometimes we open our "pop-up" Tea Rooms for a traditional and generous Afternoon Tea. For Christmas 2023 we're pulling out all the stops - a festive Christmas Afternoon Tea with all the trimmings!

Dainty, thin sandwiches with delicious christmassy fillings. Followed by freshly baked scones with jam and home made clotted cream, just like you want it. Last, but not least, a selection of traditional English Christmas treats like mince pies, Christmas cake, fudge and yule log.

Served with lots of tea brewed to perfection and poared from proper tea pots into fine cups. We also serve coffee if that is your preference.

The menu contains gluten and lactose and you may request a vegetarian alternative on your booking form
