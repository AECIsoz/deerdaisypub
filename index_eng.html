Afternoon Tea

at The Deer & Daisy

We serve a traditional British Afternoon Tea in our small tea rooms in the middle of Kivik. We bake everything ourselves and have a great selection of tea for every taste. You choose a starting time between 13:30 and 15:00 and the sitting is 90 minutes. During the summer of 2026 now take bookings on Fridays, Saturdays and Sundays from Friday 12th June.

Book here on the website or email us at info@deerdaisyinn.se.

Take Away Tea from The Deer & Daisy

Would you like to enjoy tea and scones on the beach, at home or in our lovely garden?

Your scones, jam and clotted cream is served in a carton. Choose your tea from our regular tea menu and take a real treat out in the world with you!

We don't have outdoor service but you are welcome to find a nice spot in our garden to enjoy your take away tea.

Order here on the website, email info@deerdaisyinn.se or call 076-025 17 97.
