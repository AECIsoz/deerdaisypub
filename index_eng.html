Afternoon Tea at The Deer & Daisy

This summer we will open our "pop-up" Tea Rooms again for a traditional and generous Afternoon Tea.

Dainty, thin sandwiches with delicious fillings. Followed by freshly baked scones with jam and home made clotted cream, just like you want it. Last, but not least, a piece of seasonally inspired cake.

Served with lots of tea brewed to perfection and poared from proper tea pots into fine cups. We also serve coffee if that is your preference.

The menu contains gluten and lactose and you may request a vegetarian alternative on your booking form
