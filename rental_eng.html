A home away from home

Holiday cottage at The Deer & Daisy

Would you like to holiday in the middle of Kivik with a sea view, access to a large mature garden and every convenience at your disposal?

Well, then you should really rent the Annexe at The Deer & Daisy! It's got room for six people and the beds are made when you arrive. The living area is generous on the ground floor and the upstairs bedrooms have a plush carpet, beautiful colours and a wonderful sea view.

Christmas feeling all through December 2025

From 29th November the Annexe is decorated for a sumptious English Christmas. The Christmas tree is up, the beds are made with soft flanell sheets and the electric fire is ablaze in the cosy living room.

Do you want to add Afternoon Tea to your booking? Just let us know and we will set a table for you in our Christmas decorated tea room.
